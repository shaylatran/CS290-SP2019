Astrology
The study of the movements and relative positions of celestial bodies interpreted as having an influence on human affairs and the natural world.
Compatability
a state in which two things are able to exist or occur together without problems or conflict.
Chinese Zodiac
The Chinese Zodiac, known as Sheng Xiao, is based on a twelve-year cycle, each year in that cycle related to an animal sign. These signs in order are the rat, ox, tiger, rabbit, dragon, snake, horse, sheep, monkey, rooster, dog and pig.
